About Jain's Central Park East

Jain's Central Park East is a prestigious residential project by the well-established Jain Group, located in Medchal — one of Hyderabad's fastest appreciating real estate corridors. The project offers thoughtfully designed 2 BHK and 3 BHK apartments within a fully gated, landscaped community.

Medchal's strategic position along NH-44 and near the Outer Ring Road makes it an exceptional choice for professionals working in Hitech City, Secunderabad, or the HMDA areas north of Hyderabad. The upcoming Metro corridor is expected to further boost connectivity and appreciation potential.

Jain Group has a strong track record in Hyderabad real estate with multiple successful deliveries. Central Park East continues that legacy with quality construction, green spaces, and a complete lifestyle ecosystem within the community.
