About Candeur Eternia

Candeur Eternia is Bachupally's most premium gated community, developed by Candeur Constructions. Spread across 7 acres with 4 towers, it offers meticulously designed 2 BHK, 2.5 BHK, and 3 BHK apartments ranging from 1,462 to 2,192 square feet.

What sets Candeur Eternia apart is its extraordinary amenity offering — 68,000+ sq.ft spread across two dedicated grand clubhouses. From a preview theatre and full-service restaurant to 12 guest rooms, two swimming pools, and a banquet hall, this is genuinely resort-style living in Hyderabad.

Located just 5 minutes from ORR Exit 4 and 20 minutes from Hitech City via ORR, Candeur Eternia is strategically positioned for IT professionals and families looking for premium quality without paying Gachibowli prices.
