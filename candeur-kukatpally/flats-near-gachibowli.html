Gachibowli – Hyderabad's Financial District & IT Powerhouse

Gachibowli has evolved into Hyderabad's most prominent business address. Home to the Financial District, DLF Cyber City, Wipro, Microsoft, Amazon and dozens of Fortune 500 companies, it employs over 500,000 IT and financial services professionals. The area also houses the University of Hyderabad, ISB, and IIIT — making it a complete knowledge and business ecosystem.

However, this concentration of commercial activity has made Gachibowli one of the most expensive residential markets in Hyderabad. Apartment prices in Gachibowli and the immediate Nanakramguda-Narsingi belt now range between ₹10,000 and ₹15,000 per square foot. A standard 3 BHK apartment of 1,800 sq.ft can cost anywhere from ₹1.8 to ₹2.7 crore — a significant financial commitment, especially for professionals in their early to mid-career stage.

The answer for many Gachibowli professionals lies in the Kukatpally corridor, which is connected to Gachibowli via the Outer Ring Road in just 20 minutes. This signal-free expressway route makes Candeur Kukatpally IDL Lake a practical and financially attractive alternative to Gachibowli's overheated residential market — offering larger homes, better amenities, and a greener living environment at a fraction of the price.
