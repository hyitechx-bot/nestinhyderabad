The 2.5 BHK configuration is perfect for work-from-home professionals and young couples who need a dedicated study or office space without committing to a full 3 BHK. Features include:

  • Master bedroom with attached bathroom and wardrobe
  • Second bedroom with attached/common bathroom
  • Dedicated study/work room with natural light
  • Open-plan living and dining area
  • Modular kitchen with utility area
  • Balconies with lake/garden views (select units)

3 BHK – 1,700 to 2,205 Sq.Ft

The 3 BHK is the most popular configuration, ideal for families who value space and comfort. Available in standard and large variants:

  • Three spacious bedrooms, all with wardrobes
  • Master bedroom with walk-in wardrobe (large variant)
  • 3 or 4 bathrooms depending on variant
  • Separate living and dining areas
  • Large kitchen with utility and service area
  • Servant room with attached bath (large variant)
  • Multiple balconies for lake/city views

Design Philosophy

Every apartment at Candeur Kukatpally is designed with:

  • Cross Ventilation – Windows on opposite walls for natural airflow
  • Maximum Natural Light – Large windows and open layouts
  • Vastu Compliance – Layouts aligned with Vastu principles
  • Premium Specifications – UPVC windows, vitrified tiles, HVAC provisions
  • Privacy Optimised – Minimal common walls between units

Detailed carpet area breakdowns will be available in the floor plan brochure. Contact us to receive the complete specification document with carpet and super built-up area details for each configuration.

Are there Vastu-compliant units?

Yes. Multiple units in each tower are designed with Vastu-compliant layouts including east and north-facing entrances. Speak to our team for Vastu-specific floor plan options.
