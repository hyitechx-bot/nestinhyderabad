Frequently Asked Questions – Location

How far is Candeur Kukatpally from Hitech City?

Candeur Kukatpally IDL Lake is approximately 12 km from Hitech City, reachable in about 15 minutes via the Outer Ring Road. This makes it ideal for IT professionals working in the Hitech City–Madhapur–Gachibowli belt.

Which metro station is closest?

Kukatpally Metro Station is the nearest, just 5 minutes (2.5 km) from the project. JNTU Metro Station is 8 minutes away and Miyapur Metro is 10 minutes away.

Is the project directly adjacent to IDL Lake?

Yes. Candeur Kukatpally is located adjacent to IDL Lake, offering residents serene lakefront views and a green, pollution-free environment while remaining well-connected to the city.

How is the ORR connectivity?

The Outer Ring Road is approximately 3 km (5 minutes) from the project. ORR provides seamless access to Gachibowli, Financial District, Shamshabad Airport and all major sectors of Hyderabad.
