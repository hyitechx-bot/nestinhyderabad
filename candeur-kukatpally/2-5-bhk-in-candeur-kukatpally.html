Frequently Asked Questions – 2.5 BHK

What does 2.5 BHK mean?

2.5 BHK means 2 full bedrooms + 1 smaller room (the "half" room) that serves as a study, home office or multipurpose room. It's bigger than a 2 BHK but more affordable than a 3 BHK.

What is the size range of 2.5 BHK at Candeur Kukatpally?

The 2.5 BHK apartments at Candeur Kukatpally IDL Lake range from 1,552 sq.ft to 1,650 sq.ft depending on the unit type and floor position.

Is the study room big enough for a home office?

Yes. The study/work room is a fully enclosed space (not an alcove) with enough room for a desk, bookshelf and seating. It has a door for privacy and AC provision for comfort.

Can I convert the study room into a bedroom later?

The study room has ventilation and can be used flexibly. However, it may be slightly smaller than a full bedroom. Check the floor plan dimensions to assess suitability for your needs.
