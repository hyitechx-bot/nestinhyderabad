3 BHK for Every Family Size

Candeur Kukatpally - IDL Lake offers 3 BHK apartments in two distinct variants — Standard (1,700–2,000 sq.ft) and Large (2,000–2,205 sq.ft). Whether you're a growing family needing three good-sized bedrooms or a larger household requiring a servant room and extra living space, there's a 3 BHK designed for you.

Every 3 BHK unit is crafted with family-centric principles: ample natural light, cross-ventilation, distinct zones for living, dining and sleeping, and thoughtful storage solutions throughout.

3 BHK Standard (1,700–2,000 Sq.Ft)

The standard 3 BHK is the most popular family configuration — three well-proportioned bedrooms, a spacious living-dining area, modular kitchen with utility area, and 2–3 bathrooms.

  • Master Bedroom – King-sized with walk-in wardrobe space, ensuite bathroom and balcony access
  • Second Bedroom – Full-sized children's or guest room with wardrobe and study nook
  • Third Bedroom – Versatile room for elderly parents, guests or a home office
  • Living & Dining – Open-plan design with high ceilings and floor-to-ceiling windows
  • Kitchen – L-shaped or U-shaped with utility/washing area, chimney provision and ample storage
  • Balconies – 2–3 balconies offering lake-facing or garden-facing views

3 BHK Large (2,000–2,205 Sq.Ft)

The large variant adds a servant room with attached bathroom — essential for joint families or those who employ domestic help. Additional features include a larger foyer, separate dining area, and extra storage.

  • Servant Room with Bath – Separate entry-accessible room with attached toilet and ventilation
  • Grand Living Room – Expanded living space suitable for large gatherings and family time
  • Separate Dining Area – Dedicated dining zone distinct from the living room
  • Foyer/Entrance Lobby – Welcoming entry space that adds privacy to the living area
  • 3 Bedrooms + Servant Room – All bedrooms are full-sized with wardrobe space
  • 3 Bathrooms + Servant Toilet – Master ensuite, common bath, guest bath, and servant toilet
  • Extra Storage – Dedicated store room or linen closet in the large variant

Family-Focused Design Philosophy

  • Zoned Living – Bedrooms are clustered away from the living/kitchen zone for privacy
  • Natural Ventilation – Cross-ventilation design reduces AC dependency
  • Child Safety – Balcony railings, window grills and safe electrical points as standard
  • Senior-Friendly – Anti-skid tiles in bathrooms, grab bar provisions, wide corridors
  • Community Access – Direct lift access to clubhouse level, kids' play areas visible from towers
  • Green Views – Lake-facing and garden-facing options available across towers

Specifications & Finishes

  • Doors & Windows – Flush doors with premium hardware, UPVC/aluminium windows with mosquito mesh

Frequently Asked Questions – 3 BHK

What is the size range of 3 BHK at Candeur Kukatpally?

3 BHK apartments range from 1,700 sq.ft (standard) to 2,205 sq.ft (large variant with servant room). Multiple sub-types are available within each variant.

Does the 3 BHK have a servant room?

The 3 BHK Large variants (2,000–2,205 sq.ft) include a separate servant room with an attached bathroom. Standard variants (1,700–2,000 sq.ft) do not include a servant room.

How many bathrooms are in the 3 BHK?

Standard 3 BHK units have 2–3 bathrooms (master ensuite + common). The Large variant has 3 bathrooms plus a servant toilet, totalling 4 wet areas.

Are there lake-facing 3 BHK units available?

Yes. Multiple towers offer lake-facing (IDL Lake) orientations. Lake-facing and garden-facing units are available in both standard and large variants. Contact us for available facing options.

Is there parking for 3 BHK apartments?

Yes. 3 BHK apartments come with dedicated covered parking. The large variant may include provision for two parking slots, subject to availability.
