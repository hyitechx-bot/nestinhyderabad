Why Kukatpally Remains Hyderabad's Most Sought-After Residential Hub

Kukatpally has been one of Hyderabad's most established residential micro-markets for over two decades. Its enduring appeal stems from a rare combination: mature social infrastructure, excellent metro and ORR connectivity, proximity to the IT corridor, and a thriving retail ecosystem. From families to IT professionals, Kukatpally continues to attract buyers who prioritise convenience and community.

However, most existing projects in Kukatpally are built on 2–5 acre plots with limited amenity space. The arrival of Candeur Kukatpally - IDL Lake on a 20-acre land parcel represents a generational shift — offering resort-scale living in an established locality.
