Why Madhapur Is Hyderabad's IT Heartland

Madhapur is the beating heart of Hyderabad's IT industry. Home to Cyberabad's biggest IT parks — Raheja Mindspace, Inorbit Mall zone, and dozens of startups and mid-sized tech companies — Madhapur offers what no other Hyderabad locality can: a 5-minute commute for thousands of IT professionals.

The area has evolved from a purely commercial IT corridor into a vibrant mixed-use zone with restaurants, cafes, co-working spaces, and premium residential towers. Living in or near Madhapur means eliminating the daily commute that consumes hours for professionals in other parts of the city.

Current residential prices in Madhapur range from ₹8,000 to ₹11,000 per sq.ft, reflecting its prime status. While new land availability within Madhapur is limited, several premium projects in adjacent micro-markets (Kondapur, Gachibowli, Financial District) offer Madhapur-equivalent proximity at competitive pricing.

Madhapur IT Proximity Map

  • Hitech City (Raheja Mindspace): 5 minutes — Microsoft, Qualcomm, Infosys
  • Gachibowli Financial District: 10 minutes — Wipro, Capgemini, DLF IT SEZ
  • Nanakramguda: 12 minutes — TCS, State government IT offices
  • Kondapur IT Parks: 8 minutes — HCL, Cognizant offices
  • HITEC City SEZ: 7 minutes — Amazon, Google offices

Investment Outlook: Madhapur & Surrounds 2026

Madhapur's core has limited new supply, which keeps prices firm and appreciation steady at 8–10% annually.
