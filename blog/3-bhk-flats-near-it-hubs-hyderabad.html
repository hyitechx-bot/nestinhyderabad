3 BHK Flats Near IT Hubs
Hyderabad 2026 – Best Projects & Prices

A comprehensive guide to the best 3 BHK apartments within commuting distance of Hyderabad's major IT parks — with commute times, prices, and honest recommendations.

Buying Guide • July 12, 2026 • ~11 min read

If you're an IT professional in Hyderabad looking for a 3 BHK apartment, you're part of the city's largest buyer demographic. The 3 BHK configuration is the sweet spot for technology workers — enough space for a family, a dedicated home office, and room to grow — and Hyderabad offers some of India's best options within striking distance of major IT campuses.

This guide compares the best 3 BHK options across Hyderabad's IT corridor, with real commute data, pricing breakdowns, and clear recommendations for different buyer profiles.

Why IT Professionals Choose 3 BHK

The shift to 3 BHK among Hyderabad's IT workforce is driven by several converging factors:

  • Work from home: Even with return-to-office, most IT companies maintain hybrid policies. A dedicated home office (the third bedroom) is no longer a luxury — it's a necessity.
  • Growing families: IT professionals in the 28–40 age bracket are typically starting or growing families, needing the extra bedroom for children.
  • Affordability: Unlike Bangalore where a 3 BHK near IT hubs costs ₹2–3 Crore, Hyderabad offers comparable properties at ₹1–1.8 Crore — making 3 BHK achievable on a dual-income IT salary.
  • Investment value: 3 BHK apartments appreciate faster and command stronger rental demand than smaller configurations in the IT belt.

Best 3 BHK Options by IT Hub

If You Work in Hitech City (Microsoft, Google, Infosys)

Hitech City is Hyderabad's densest IT employment zone, housing campuses of Microsoft, Google, Infosys, TCS, and dozens of mid-sized firms. The best 3 BHK options for Hitech City workers:

  • IDL Lake - Kukatpally – 15 min drive, Metro option available, established area
  • Eternia – 20 min via ORR, best amenities and space
  • Skyline – 22 min via ORR, premium positioning in Tellapur

Our recommendation: If you drive, Eternia offers the best combination of commute, space, and value. If you prefer Metro, IDL Lake - Kukatpally gives you the shortest commute with Metro backup.

If You Work in Gachibowli (Amazon, Deloitte, DBS)

Gachibowli hosts Amazon's largest India campus, Deloitte, DBS, Qualcomm, and the growing Knowledge City development. Best 3 BHK picks:

  • Skyline – 15 min via ORR, closest project to Gachibowli
  • Lakescape – 18 min, nature-oriented alternative in same corridor
  • Eternia – 30 min via ORR, best value if willing to trade 15 extra minutes

Our recommendation: Skyline for those prioritising commute; Eternia for those prioritising space and amenities over a slightly longer drive.

If You Work in Madhapur (Apple, Accenture, Wipro)

Madhapur sits between Hitech City and Gachibowli, hosting Apple, Accenture, Wipro, and numerous startups. Best options:

  • IDL Lake - Kukatpally – 18 min drive, Metro connectivity
  • Skyline – 20 min via ORR
  • Eternia – 25 min via ORR, superior value proposition

If You Work in Financial District (TCS, Capgemini, State Street)

The Financial District (Nanakramguda) is the newest IT zone, growing rapidly with TCS, Capgemini, State Street, and multiple upcoming campuses. Best picks:

  • Skyline – 18 min, optimal for Financial District commuters
  • Lakescape – 20 min, lake-facing alternative
  • Eternia – 35 min via ORR, viable for hybrid workers (2–3 days in office)

Which Project Suits Which Buyer Profile?

The Family Upgrader (Budget ₹1.3–1.8 Cr)

You're in your mid-30s, have 1–2 children, and want a spacious 3 BHK in a community with great amenities and schools nearby. Your priority is quality of life, not the shortest commute.

Best choice: Eternia — the 1,790–2,100 sq.ft 3 BHK with 68,000 sq.ft amenities (children's areas, swimming pool, sports facilities) is built exactly for this profile. DPS and Oakridge schools are within 10 minutes.

The Young Professional (Budget ₹1.0–1.2 Cr)

You're 28–32, recently married or soon to be, buying your first 3 BHK. You want gated community living but don't need the largest apartment. Metro connectivity matters for flexibility.

Best choice: C40 or Twins — the 1,400–1,550 sq.ft 3 BHK gives you enough space without over-stretching your budget. Metro proximity adds lifestyle flexibility and resale value.

The Premium Buyer (Budget ₹1.5–2.0 Cr)

You're in a senior role, value premium finishes and a prestigious address. You work in Gachibowli or Financial District and want the shortest possible commute with no compromise on quality.

Best choice: Skyline (Tellapur) — the 1,800–2,100 sq.ft 3 BHK with luxury amenities, 15-minute commute to Gachibowli, and premium Tellapur address matches this profile perfectly.

The Smart Investor (Budget ₹1.0–1.5 Cr)

You're buying primarily for investment — rental income and appreciation. You want the best possible returns over 5–7 years.

Best choice: Crescent — pre-launch pricing gives you 15–20% built-in appreciation before possession. The Bachupally corridor's growth trajectory means strong post-possession appreciation too. Alternatively, Eternia for ready-to-rent units.

The Nature Lover (Budget ₹1.2–1.6 Cr)

You want green surroundings, open spaces, and a calmer living environment. You don't mind a slightly longer commute if it means coming home to serenity.

Best choice: Lakescape (Tellapur) — lake-facing units, nature-integrated design, and wellness-oriented amenities. The 1,650–1,900 sq.ft 3 BHK offers generous space in a green setting.

Key Amenities to Look For in a 3 BHK Gated Community

When evaluating 3 BHK projects near IT hubs, these amenities directly impact your daily quality of life:

  • Swimming pool: Essential for families — look for separate children's pool
  • Gymnasium: Full-equipped gym saves you a club membership (₹30,000–50,000/year)
  • Children's play areas: Multiple age-appropriate zones for under-5 and 5–12 groups
  • Jogging/walking track: Perimeter tracks of 400m+ for daily exercise without leaving the community
  • Indoor games: Table tennis, badminton, squash — important for rainy season and evenings
  • Party hall/community space: For birthday parties, festivals, and social gatherings
  • Power backup: Full DG backup is non-negotiable in Hyderabad's summer
  • Security: 3-tier security with CCTV, intercom, and visitor management system

Eternia leads on amenities with its 68,000 sq.ft facility that includes all of the above plus a mini theatre, rooftop lounge, restaurant, and cricket practice nets — the most comprehensive amenity package of any 3 BHK project in the western corridor.

3 BHK Loan Eligibility – Quick Guide

For IT professionals, here's a rough guide to loan eligibility for different 3 BHK price points:

  • ₹1.0–1.2 Cr property: Combined household income of ₹1.5–2.0 Lakh/month (EMI: ₹65,000–₹80,000)
  • ₹1.3–1.5 Cr property: Combined household income of ₹2.0–2.5 Lakh/month (EMI: ₹85,000–₹1,00,000)
  • ₹1.5–1.8 Cr property: Combined household income of ₹2.5–3.0 Lakh/month (EMI: ₹1,00,000–₹1,20,000)

Most banks offer 80% LTV for properties under ₹75 Lakh and 75% for properties above, so keep 20–25% ready as down payment plus registration charges (approximately 7.5% of property value in Telangana).

Final Recommendation

For most IT professionals in Hyderabad looking for a 3 BHK in 2026, Eternia in Bachupally offers the best overall package: the largest 3 BHK apartments (up to 2,100 sq.ft), the best amenities (68,000 sq.ft), strong ORR connectivity (20 min to Hitech City), and competitive pricing (₹1.3–1.8 Cr). It's the project where most IT buyers will get the most value for their investment.

For budget-conscious buyers under ₹1.2 Crore, C40 and Twins offer well-designed 3 BHK options with Metro connectivity. For Gachibowli/Financial District workers, Skyline in Tellapur provides the shortest commute with premium quality.
