Candeur Kukatpally IDL Lake Pre-Launch
Everything You Need to Know | 2026

20-acre gated community with 13 towers, G+25 floors, and a 1.35 lakh sq.ft clubhouse — Phase 1 bookings now open.

Pre-Launch Alert • July 1, 2026 • ~8 min read

Candeur Group has officially announced the pre-launch of their most ambitious project to date — Candeur Kukatpally IDL Lake. Spread across 20 acres adjacent to IDL Lake in Kukatpally, this is set to become one of Hyderabad's largest and most well-connected gated communities. With Phase 1 bookings now open, here's everything you need to know about what the project offers, why the location matters, and how to secure your unit at pre-launch pricing.

Project Overview: What Is Candeur Kukatpally IDL Lake?

Candeur Kukatpally IDL Lake is a large-format residential development designed to offer lakeside living with urban convenience. The key project specifications are:

  • Total Land Area: 20 acres
  • Number of Towers: 13 towers
  • Tower Height: G+25 floors each
  • Unit Configurations: 2.5 BHK and 3 BHK apartments
  • Unit Sizes: 1,552 sq.ft to 2,205 sq.ft
  • Clubhouse: 1.35 lakh sq.ft (one of the largest in Hyderabad)
  • Developer: Candeur Group (known for Candeur Eternia in Bachupally)

The project is positioned as a premium gated community offering generous apartment sizes, lake-facing views from multiple towers, and an amenity infrastructure that rivals luxury resorts. With 13 towers spread across 20 acres, the built density remains comfortable — expect meaningful open space, landscaped gardens, and breathing room between structures.

Location Advantage: Why Kukatpally IDL Lake?

The Kukatpally micro-market has always been one of Hyderabad's most sought-after residential zones, and this project's specific location amplifies that appeal significantly. Here's what makes the site exceptional:

  • Adjacent to IDL Lake: Direct lake views from multiple towers. The lake provides a permanent green buffer and ensures no future construction can block the view or reduce open space perception.
  • Kukatpally Metro Station: Just 5 minutes away, giving residents direct metro connectivity to Hitech City, Ameerpet, and the broader Blue Line network.
  • Outer Ring Road (ORR): 5 minutes to the nearest ORR interchange, connecting you to the entire city — Gachibowli in 12 minutes, Airport in 35 minutes.
  • Hitech City: 15-minute drive via ORR or direct metro ride — ideal for IT professionals working in the western IT corridor.
  • Established Social Infrastructure: Kukatpally already has top schools (Delhi Public School, Oakridge), hospitals (KIMS, Continental), and malls (Forum Mall, Manjeera Mall) within a 5–10 minute radius.

Unlike emerging areas where you're betting on future infrastructure, Kukatpally is a mature locality. You move in and everything — from groceries to gyms to hospitals — is already in place.

The 1.35 Lakh Sq.Ft Clubhouse

The headline amenity number is staggering: 1,35,000 sq.ft of clubhouse and amenity space. To put that in context, most "premium" gated communities in Hyderabad offer 30,000–50,000 sq.ft of amenity space. Candeur Kukatpally is delivering nearly 3x that benchmark.

While the full amenity list will be confirmed closer to launch, based on the developer's track record and project positioning, expect:

  • Olympic-standard swimming pool
  • Fully equipped gymnasium with dedicated yoga and aerobics studios
  • Indoor badminton, squash, and table tennis courts
  • Outdoor tennis and basketball courts
  • Multi-purpose banquet hall and party area
  • Children's play zones (indoor and outdoor)
  • Landscaped jogging track and cycling path
  • Co-working spaces and business lounge
  • Mini theatre and gaming zone
  • Senior citizen dedicated areas with walking paths

Unit Configurations and Sizes

The project offers two primary configurations designed for families who need spacious, functional homes:

Configuration Size Range Ideal For Key Feature
2.5 BHK 1,552 – 1,680 sq.ft Young couples, small families Extra half room works as home office or nursery
3 BHK 1,850 – 2,205 sq.ft Growing families, professionals needing home office Spacious living + separate dining area

All units are designed with cross-ventilation, large balconies (many with lake views), modular kitchen-ready layouts, and 3-side open configurations in corner units. The 2.5 BHK format is particularly interesting — it gives you the extra room flexibility of a 3 BHK in a more compact, affordable footprint.

Pre-Launch Benefits: Why Book Now?

Pre-launch is the window where early buyers get the best combination of pricing and choice. Here's what Candeur Kukatpally is offering during the pre-launch phase:

  • Early-Bird Pricing: Pre-launch prices are typically 10–15% below launch-day rates. As construction progresses and demand solidifies, prices will step up with each phase.
  • Priority Floor and Unit Selection: Early bookers get first pick of floors, tower positions, and lake-facing units. In a 13-tower project, the difference between a lake-view unit and an internal-facing unit is significant in both lifestyle and resale value.
  • Price Lock with Expression of Interest (EOI): You can lock in the current price with a nominal EOI amount. This protects you from any price revision between now and the formal launch.
  • Flexible Payment Plans: Pre-launch buyers typically get the most favourable payment structures — lower upfront commitments and construction-linked plans that align payments with project milestones.

Who Should Book at Candeur Kukatpally IDL Lake?

This project is best suited for:

  • IT professionals working in Hitech City/Gachibowli: 15-minute commute via ORR or metro makes this an ideal home base without paying Kondapur/Gachibowli prices.
  • Families wanting established infrastructure: Schools, hospitals, malls, and public transport are all already operational in Kukatpally — no waiting 3–5 years for the area to develop.
  • Investors seeking appreciation: Lakeside property in a mature market with metro connectivity is a proven formula for strong capital appreciation. Limited lake-adjacent land ensures scarcity value.
  • Buyers upgrading from older Kukatpally apartments: If you already live in Kukatpally and want to upgrade to a modern gated community without changing your children's schools or daily routines.
