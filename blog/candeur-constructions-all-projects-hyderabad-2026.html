Candeur Constructions has emerged as one of Hyderabad's most active residential developers, with eight projects across the city's western growth corridor. About Candeur Constructions

Company Background

Candeur Constructions is a Hyderabad-based real estate developer focused on premium gated community apartments in the city's western corridor. The company has built its reputation on delivering larger-than-average apartments with extensive amenity packages at competitive price points.

Track Record

  • Projects launched: 8 residential projects (7 in Hyderabad, 1 in Bangalore)
  • Focus areas: Bachupally, Miyapur, Kukatpally, Tellapur
  • USP: Large amenity footprints (50,000–70,000+ sq.ft), spacious apartments, ORR-connected locations
  • RERA status: All projects RERA-registered under Telangana RERA
  • Construction quality: Mivan shuttering technology, earthquake-resistant design, branded fittings

Delivery History

Candeur's earlier projects have been delivered with generally positive buyer feedback on construction quality and amenity delivery. The builder has maintained a track record of delivering what was promised in terms of specifications and amenity scope. Delivery timelines have been within acceptable ranges for the Hyderabad market.

1. Candeur Eternia – Bachupally (Flagship Project)

Overview

Candeur Eternia is Candeur's flagship project and the one that established the brand's reputation in Hyderabad. Spread across 7 acres in Bachupally, it features one of the largest amenity packages in the city at 68,000+ sq.ft of recreational facilities.

Key Details

  • Location: Bachupally, near ORR Exit 4 (Mallampet Interchange)
  • Configurations: 2 BHK (1,190 sq.ft), 2.5 BHK (1,400 sq.ft), 3 BHK (1,790–2,100 sq.ft)
  • Price range: ₹1.2 Crore – ₹1.8 Crore
  • Total units: 500+ apartments across multiple towers
  • Amenities: 68,000 sq.ft including swimming pool, gym, indoor games, mini theatre, rooftop lounge, restaurant, cricket practice nets, jogging track, children's play areas
  • Connectivity: 5 min to ORR, 20 min to Hitech City, 12 min to Miyapur Metro
  • RERA: Registered
  • Status: Under construction, possession expected 2027

Who Should Buy

IT professionals working in Hitech City/Gachibowli who want maximum space and amenities for their budget. Families with children who value large community living. Investors looking for the strongest appreciation potential in the Bachupally corridor.

2. Candeur C40 – Miyapur

Overview

Candeur C40 is positioned as a compact luxury project in Miyapur, targeting young professionals and first-time buyers who want gated community living near the Metro at an accessible price point.

Key Details

  • Location: Miyapur, near Miyapur Metro Station
  • Configurations: 2 BHK (1,050–1,150 sq.ft), 2.5 BHK (1,250 sq.ft), 3 BHK (1,450–1,550 sq.ft)
  • Price range: ₹70 Lakh – ₹1.2 Crore
  • USP: Metro proximity (walkable distance to Miyapur Metro), compact efficient layouts, modern amenities
  • Amenities: Swimming pool, gymnasium, clubhouse, children's play area, landscaped gardens, indoor games
  • Connectivity: Walkable to Miyapur Metro, 5 min to ORR, 25 min to Hitech City
  • RERA: Registered
  • Status: Under construction

Who Should Buy

Young IT professionals buying their first home. Those who rely on Metro for daily commuting. Budget-conscious buyers who want gated community living under ₹1 Crore. Investors targeting high rental demand from the Miyapur IT corridor workforce.

Candeur Constructions – Final Assessment

Candeur has carved out a niche in Hyderabad's competitive real estate market by consistently delivering projects that offer more space and amenities at a given price point than many established competitors. Their focus on the western corridor — from Bachupally through Miyapur and Kukatpally to Tellapur — positions them well to benefit from the continued westward expansion of Hyderabad's IT economy.

For buyers, the key differentiators are: larger apartment sizes than market average, extensive amenity packages (particularly in Eternia), competitive pricing for gated community living, and locations with strong ORR connectivity. The primary consideration is that Candeur is a relatively newer brand compared to legacy developers, though their delivery track record to date has been positive.
