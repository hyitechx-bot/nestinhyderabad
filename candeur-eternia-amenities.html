Why 68,000 Sq.Ft Matters

68,000 square feet is roughly the size of a full-sized football pitch and a half. It's not a number that fits easily in the imagination until you start comparing it to what other projects in Bachupally — and even premium areas like Kondapur — actually offer.

Most gated communities in the ₹80L–₹1.5Cr range in Bachupally offer a single clubhouse of 8,000–15,000 sq.ft. A standard gym, a small pool, a few courts, and a basic hall. That's it. A private gym membership in Hyderabad runs ₹3,000–₹8,000 per month. A 5-star spa session is ₹3,000–₹5,000. A private screening at a multiplex costs ₹5,000+. A guest room at a business hotel near Hitech City is ₹4,000–₹6,000 per night.

At Candeur Eternia, all of this is included in your monthly maintenance. When you have family visiting from another city, they stay in the guest rooms downstairs instead of a hotel. When you want a movie night, the preview theatre is available to book. When you need a business meeting space, the business centre is downstairs.

For projects in the ₹1.5–₹2Cr range in Kondapur that offer comparable prices, the amenity offering rarely exceeds 20,000–25,000 sq.ft. Candeur Eternia, at ₹1.2Cr starting, offers nearly three times that. The amenity-to-price ratio here is genuinely exceptional.
