Flats Near Hitech City, Hyderabad
Complete Location & Price Guide 2026

A data-driven guide to every option for IT professionals buying near Hitech City — from 5-minute walks to 22-minute ORR commutes, with full price comparisons.

💼 IT Professionals Guide • Updated June 2026 •

Hitech City: India's Most Important IT Hub Address

Hitech City in Hyderabad is not just a technology park — it's one of the highest concentration of IT employment in the country. With over 3 lakh IT professionals commuting to the Hitech City–Madhapur–Kondapur corridor daily, and top employers including Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Deloitte, Infosys, TCS, and hundreds of product and services companies occupying the space, demand for residential proximity to this hub is structural and permanent.

For IT professionals buying their first home or upgrading, the key question is always: how close do I need to be, and what does that proximity actually cost? This guide breaks it down by area, commute time, and price per sq.ft.

Areas Near Hitech City: Proximity vs Price

Madhapur: 5 Min · ₹11,000–₹15,000/sq.ft

Walking or cycling distance from Hitech City itself. Premium pricing reflects the absolute proximity. Apartment inventory is limited and mostly older stock; few new launches given land scarcity. Best for senior professionals prioritising zero commute above all else. 2 BHK starts at ₹1.6–₹2 Crore.

Kondapur: 10 Min · ₹9,000–₹11,000/sq.ft

The most popular choice for Hitech City professionals. Excellent social infrastructure — restaurants, clinics, gyms, malls — and 10-minute commute via surface roads on a good day (15–20 min in peak hours). Many established gated communities. 2 BHK typically ₹1.3–₹1.8 Crore.

Gachibowli: 12 Min · ₹10,500–₹13,000/sq.ft

Adjacent to Hitech City and the Financial District. Gachibowli's own IT presence means buyers here serve two major employment hubs simultaneously. Premium pricing, premium product, but limited new inventory at below ₹2 Crore.

Kukatpally via ORR: 18 Min · ₹7,000–₹8,500/sq.ft

The ORR connection from Kukatpally to Hitex makes this corridor accessible. Well-established social infrastructure, IKEA, Forum Sujana Mall, and strong metro connectivity via KPHB. Pricing is 25–35% below Kondapur for comparable specs.

Bachupally via ORR: 20–25 Min · ₹6,500–₹11,000/sq.ft

Here's the value case that most buyers overlook. Bachupally via ORR Exit 4 delivers a 20–25-minute commute to Hitech City for IT professionals — just 5–15 minutes more than Kondapur on surface roads, and on some peak-hour days, faster than Kondapur via congested internal roads. Pricing starts at ₹6,500/sq.ft and reaches ₹11,000/sq.ft for premium projects. The pricing gap vs Kondapur is 30–40% for a commute difference of 5–10 minutes.
