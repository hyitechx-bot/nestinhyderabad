About Jain's Central Park East

Jain's Central Park East is a thoughtfully designed gated community in Uppal,Narapally by the established Jain Group. The project is positioned for buyers seeking value on Hyderabad's northern NH-44 corridor — offering spacious 2 and 3 BHK apartments within a green, secure community at pricing well below comparable projects closer to the city centre.

The Jain Group has a consistent track record of delivering RERA-compliant projects in Hyderabad. Central Park East continues this with quality construction, mature landscaping, and amenities designed around families and professionals who want space and greenery at a sensible price.
