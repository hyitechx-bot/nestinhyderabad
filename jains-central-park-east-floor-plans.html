Jain's Central Park East – Layout Design Notes

🌿

Green-Facing Layouts

Most units are oriented to face the central park or landscaped zones, ensuring natural greenery views from living rooms and master bedrooms.

🌬️

Cross-Ventilation Design

Layouts are designed for through-ventilation, keeping interiors naturally cooler — important for Medchal's slightly more open, less-urban microclimate.

📐

Efficient Space Planning

Jain Group's floor plan philosophy prioritises usable carpet area. The 2 BHK at 1,450 sq.ft delivers more actual living space than many builders' 1,600 sq.ft units due to minimal wall thickness waste.

🚗

Generous Parking

2-wheeler and 4-wheeler covered parking included. Medchal's plot sizes allow for better basement parking design compared to cramped city-centre projects.
