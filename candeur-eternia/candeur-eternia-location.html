Where is Candeur Eternia?

Candeur Eternia is located in Bachupally, Hyderabad, Telangana – 500090, near Astra Lumina School on Miyapur Road. The project sits just off Miyapur Extension, one of Hyderabad's fastest-growing residential corridors. It offers seamless access to the Outer Ring Road (ORR), Hitech City, Gachibowli and the broader city — while remaining in a quieter, greener neighbourhood away from the urban rush.

Bachupally has emerged as a preferred address for IT professionals, young families and investors alike, thanks to its excellent connectivity, established social infrastructure and relatively affordable land prices compared to Hitech City or Kondapur.

Why Bachupally is the Right Address

Bachupally offers a rare combination — the calm of a residential neighbourhood with the connectivity of a prime location.
