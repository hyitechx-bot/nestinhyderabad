Nature-First Living in East Bengaluru

Candeur Novo Gardenia is a premium gated community located in East Bengaluru's growing Varthur/Gunjur corridor. The project stands out for its strong focus on greenery, landscaping and open spaces — offering a nature-first lifestyle for modern families.

With well-designed 2 & 3 BHK apartments and prices starting at ₹80 Lakhs, this is an excellent opportunity for buyers looking for quality living in one of Bengaluru's most promising growth corridors, close to Whitefield and Sarjapur Road IT hubs.
