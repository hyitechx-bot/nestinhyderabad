Why Candeur Lakescape?

A stunning lake-view gated community in Kondapur, near University of Hyderabad, offering an exceptional lifestyle at competitive pricing.

Lake Views, Premium Living

Candeur Lakescape is one of Kondapur's most iconic developments, offering breathtaking lake views and premium living spaces. Spread across 9.11 acres with 7 towers rising 47 floors, this project combines nature, luxury and connectivity.

The 1,00,000 sq.ft clubhouse is among the largest in Hyderabad, providing world-class amenities for residents of all ages. With configurations from 2 BHK to 3.5 BHK and sizes from 1,296 to 2,416 sq.ft, there's something for every family.
