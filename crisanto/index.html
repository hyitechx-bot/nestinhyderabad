About the Project

Why Crisanto?

An iconic ultra-luxury gated community by Avanthika Constructions in Ameenpur-Chandanagar.

Ultra Luxury Living by Avanthika Constructions

Crisanto is an iconic ultra-luxury gated community spread across 24.5 acres in the thriving Ameenpur-Chandanagar corridor of Hyderabad. Featuring 7 high-rise towers with 39 floors each, it offers spacious 2, 2.5, 3 & 4 BHK apartments ranging from 1,355 to 3,475 sq.ft.

HMDA approved and 100% Vaastu compliant, Crisanto is just 2 minutes from the upcoming BHEL Metro station and close to Lingampally Railway Station. With a 1,25,000 sq.ft sports arena and two clubhouses totalling 1,00,000 sq.ft, it redefines luxury living in Hyderabad's fastest-growing western corridor.
