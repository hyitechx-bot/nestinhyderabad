Floor Plan Design Philosophy

🌅

Maximum Natural Light

East-facing units get morning sunlight in the living area. West-facing units are designed for cross-ventilation, keeping interiors cooler through Hyderabad summers.

📐

No Dead Spaces

Every square foot is accounted for — from wide living+dining open plans to utility rooms that handle the practicalities most apartments ignore.

💼

Home Office Ready

The 2.5 BHK study room and 3 BHK third bedroom both work as dedicated home offices with natural light — a design priority in the hybrid work era.

🏗️

Shear Wall Construction

RCC Monolithic Shear Wall construction means fewer columns inside your home. Cleaner interiors, larger usable spaces, and superior structural strength.
