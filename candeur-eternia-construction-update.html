How to Track RERA Progress Independently

Candeur Eternia is RERA registered under number P02200010709 in Telangana. You can verify construction progress, possession dates, and developer compliance directly on the RERA portal.

  1. Visit rera.telangana.gov.in
  2. Click on "Project Search" or "Registered Projects"
  3. Enter RERA number P02200010709
  4. View project details, developer submissions, and quarterly progress reports
  5. Check the possession date filed with RERA vs. developer's stated timeline

💡 RERA mandates that developers file quarterly construction updates. If a developer fails to file or misses deadlines, buyers can file complaints directly through the RERA portal.

Construction Quality Highlights

  • 🏗️ RCC Monolithic Shear Wall Construction: Superior seismic resistance compared to conventional column-beam structures. Fewer interior columns means cleaner, more spacious interiors.
  • 📏 2.985m Floor Height (Clear): Noticeably spacious feel. Most competing projects offer 2.7–2.8m — the difference is perceptible once you walk through.
  • 🪟 UPVC Windows with Double Glazing: Better thermal insulation and noise reduction, relevant for Hyderabad's summer temperatures.
  • 🧱 AAC Block Walls: Autoclaved Aerated Concrete blocks offer better insulation and are lighter than conventional brick — reducing structural load while improving energy efficiency.
  • 🚿 Premium Bathroom Fittings: Kohler / equivalent branded sanitary fittings. Anti-skid tiles in bathrooms as standard.
  • 🔌 Concealed Conduit Wiring: All electrical wiring concealed within walls with sufficient power points per room for modern usage including home office setups.
